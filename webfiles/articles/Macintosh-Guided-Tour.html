= Macintosh 128k Guided Tour = '' '''NOTE:''' This demo may crash occasionally'' { "icons" : [ ["A Guided Tour of Macintosh", "boot-floppy", "/disks/mac-s.85-f1.0-tour.image", {"emulator-args" : {"-c" : "pce-mac-128k.cfg"} } ] ] } This is the Guided Tour from the 128K Macintosh. The audio cassette provided narration while the user performed exercises and watched demonstrations on their Mac's screen. Thanks to Mac128DOTcom's excellent YouTube collection, you can experience what it was like to use the Guided Tour cassette. Listen to these videos while you follow along in the emulated Macintosh; or, simply sit back and watch the videos as they take you through the entire tour! { "icons" : [ ["Mac128DOTcom Videos", "hyperlink", "https://www.youtube.com/user/Mac128DOTcom"] ] } # [https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=1pwammW5syw Intro & "Mousing Around"] # [https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=hAilgGS-JHs "Show Me My Electronic Desk!"] # [https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=EsZxIP08uWE "Let Me Use My Macintosh!"] # [https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=tXhXQNDYJqg "What's The Finder"] # [https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=rLX4TW9WRco "Why Do I Have Windows"] # [https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=YEui4C1TlNY "What Else Is In The Menus"] # [https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=VbGl9-Kh2oE "Time To Play The Maze!" and "I'm Ready To Stop!"] == Details to notice == If you click "Let Me Use My Macintosh" from this disk, you can explore a very early version of the Finder.
