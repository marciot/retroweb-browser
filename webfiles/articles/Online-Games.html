= Multi-Player Online Games = In the days before broadband, computers used dial-up modems to communicate with one another. Some early games made use of this technology to allow people to play games together. This web page emulates a computer with a modem, allowing you to play games with other people who visit this web page! You and your opponent will need a virtual telephone number to connect to one another. == MazeWars+ == This game lets you hunt down your opponent in a three-dimensional maze. MazeWars+ was released in 1986 and is very capable for the time. You can pick custom avatars, select from multiple robotic side-kicks and communicate with your opponent via text messages. To connect to another player: { "icons" : [ ["MazeWars+", "boot-floppy", "/disks/mac-mazewars-plus-1.1.dsk"] ] } * Get a virtual phone number above and then click the MazeWars+ floppy. * Enter your name, and select your avatar. * Click the Phone icon, or select "Phone..." from the "Options" menu. * Instruct a friend to visit this page on their own computer and do the same. * You check the "Auto Answer" checkbox and click "OK" * Your friend type in your virtual phone number and clicks "Dial" * Select "About MazeWars+..." from the Apple menu to learn the keyboard controls. == ZTerm == { "icons" : [ ["Boot Disk", "boot-hd", "/disks/mac-marcio's-boot-1.0.qed"], ["ZTerm", "floppy", "/disks/mac-zterm-0.9.img"] ] } Or, use ZTerm to interact with the modem, chat with one another, or transfer files the primitive way!
